What is EdenStore?

EdenStore is a no-nonsense online marketplace built exclusively for Maseno University students. No sign-ups. No ads. No outsiders. Just a smooth, clean platform where students buy and sell anything—from phones, laptops, and books to shoes, bags, and random treasures in your hostel.

It’s built on trust, proximity, and simplicity. You see it. You want it. You call the seller. Boom—deal done.

Why EdenStore?

Because not everyone wants to scroll through WhatsApp chaos or get ghosted by random “buyers” in Facebook groups.

EdenStore brings sanity, speed, and structure to student hustles—by students, for students.

What Makes It Different?

  • Local Only – Everything’s in Maseno. No location confusion.
  • No Accounts – You don’t need a username to hustle.
  • Student Vibes – No grown-ups, no corporations, no spam.
  • Fast Posting – Upload your item in seconds.
  • Always Online – Hosted freely and forever via GitHub Pages.
